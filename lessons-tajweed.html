Tajweed is the set of rules governing the proper pronunciation and recitation of the Qur'an. The word "Tajweed" literally means "to improve" or "to make better." Learning Tajweed ensures that we recite the words of Allah Almighty exactly as they were revealed to Prophet Muhammad ï·º.

Why is Tajweed important?

  • It preserves the original pronunciation of the Qur'an
  • It beautifies the recitation and makes it melodious
  • It prevents mistakes that could alter the meaning of verses
  • It helps us recite with proper respect and reverence

What you'll learn: Arabic letter articulation points (Makharij), characteristics of letters (Sifaat), rules of Noon Sakin and Tanween, rules of Meem Sakin, Qalqalah, Madd (elongation), and much more.
