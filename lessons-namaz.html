How to Pray (Namaz)

Step-by-step guide according to Hanafi Fiqh

ðŸ•Œ Introduction to Salah (Prayer)

Salah (prayer) is the second pillar of Islam and the most important act of worship. It is the direct connection between a Muslim and Allah, performed five times daily at prescribed times.

This comprehensive guide follows the Hanafi madhab (school of thought), one of the four major Sunni schools of Islamic jurisprudence. The Hanafi madhab is known for its detailed methodology and is followed by the majority of Muslims worldwide.

What you'll learn: Complete step-by-step instructions for performing Wudu (ablution), the conditions of prayer, how to pray each of the five daily prayers, essential duas and recitations, and common mistakes to avoid.

ðŸ“Œ Note: While this guide follows Hanafi fiqh, the core of Salah is the same across all madhabs. Minor differences exist in hand placement, recitations, and specific rulings, but all are valid ways of worshipping Allah.
